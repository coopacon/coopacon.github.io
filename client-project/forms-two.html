Basics of project management skills


When I'm asked to get work done, I think about: Definition of the scope of work: What will be done? How many people are needed? Definition of the schedule: When does the work need to be done? Definition of quality: When the work is done well, how will we measure that and agree that it is working well?

Meeting management


When working with a group, I am thoughtful about: What is the goal of the meeting? Communication of information? Problem Solving? Working session? Who needs to participate? Agenda: What is a good list of topics to cover? How much time is needed for each? Schedule: When should the meeting happen in order to support everyone's goals? Facilitation: How can I help everyone participate and accomplish the meeting goal?
